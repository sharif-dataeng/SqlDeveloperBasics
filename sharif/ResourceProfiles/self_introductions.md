# Self Introductions

## Akhil Pacha

I'm Akhil Pacha having 4.3 years of IT experience, specialized in Azure Data Engineering for the past 4.3 years. My working experience includes Spark, PySpark, SQL, Databricks, ADF, ADLS, Azure Functions, and Logic App. I am working with Church & Dwight client, they are operating in Consumer Goods domain. Currently, CHD Cloud project utilizes Spotfire application developed in Tibco software.

In a strategic move, the client has opted to move all these processes to a cloud-based solution. As part of this initiative, we've developed configuration-driven pipelines and workflows to handle near-real-time data processing from the ADLS location using Autoloader in Databricks. ADF is used for data extraction and orchestration of pipelines, Databricks is used for business transformations, and Logic Apps for email notifications. We get the source data from different external sources in different formats like JSON, CSV and from on-prem DB2-400 and SAP HANA. Our project architecture follows the Medallion approach (Bronze, Silver, Gold) to meet our specific business requirements. We store the processed data in Delta Lake tables, allowing seamless integration with Power BI for report generation.
